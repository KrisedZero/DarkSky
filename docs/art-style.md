Defines the game's visual and audio aesthetic guidelines.

Theme: Dark fantasy, medieval horror. The mood should be oppressive and uncanny.
Graphics Style: Pixel art (consistent sprite resolution, e.g. 16×16 or 32×32). No high-res or smooth art.
Color Palette: Muted and desaturated colors – grays, browns, dark greens, cold blues.
Lighting: Strong contrast. Use warm light (orange) from lanterns/torches against cool shadows.
Materials: Stone walls, wooden floors and doors, iron grates. Furniture (beds, wardrobes, tables) look old and worn.
Decoration: Tapestries, paintings (dark themes), dusty books, dripping candles, cobwebs, carpets in hallways.
Characters: Boy hero (simple palette clothing), ghosts/monsters (eerie silhouettes), merchant ghost (translucent).
UI Style: Minimal HUD; dark background bars, simple pixel font, white/yellow text. Buttons are pixel-style with hover effects.
Animations: Subtle – flickering torch light, rain on roof, monster idle swaying, heartbeat pulse effect.
Audio: Low-fi chiptune or ambient horror music. SFX: dripping water, distant monster groans, footsteps (echo), heart beat (in stereo).
(Ensure all assets match this style. No modern or bright/neon colors.)
